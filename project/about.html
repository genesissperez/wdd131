Our History

Latitud Español was born from a profound observation of how the digital world is changing education. Founded in Venezuela, a vibrant cultural bridge in Latin America, this project began with a singular mission: to transform the way adults connect with the Spanish language. In geography, a latitude is a precise coordinate that defines a position on Earth. For us, that coordinate represents a global meeting point where students from entirely different background realities, career paths, and cultures converge under a shared goal.

We designed Latitud Español specifically for adult learners who need more than just survival phrases or gamified streaks. Our target students are professionals, university candidates, and global citizens who need to understand the cultural nuances, the structural grammar, and the real-world flow of Spanish. We recognized that entering a new linguistic world as an adult can be intimidating, and standard software completely ignores the emotional and psychological aspects of learning
